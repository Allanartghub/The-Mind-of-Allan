The Challenge

"Financial Exclusion Stifles Growth for Millions of Small Businesses in Emerging Markets."

Traditional lenders often have rigid requirements, demand collateral, and require a formal credit history, barriers that most smallholder farmers and micro-entrepreneurs cannot overcome. As a result, many are locked out of the financial system, unable to access the credit they need to grow, invest, or weather tough seasons. This stifles business growth, limits job creation, and perpetuates cycles of poverty.

Did you know? An estimated $330 billion SME financing gap exists in Sub-Saharan Africa alone.

Design Principles & Approach

  • User-Centric Design: Prioritized ease of use for entrepreneurs with varying digital literacy.
